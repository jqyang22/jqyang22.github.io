Research Interest

 I am working in AI for Earth Observation. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Hyperspectral image interpretation
  • Multi-modal data analysis
  • Weakly-supervised learning
My research aims to design creative machine learning and computer vision approaches to better contribute to geoscientific fields such as land cover/crop mapping, agricultural monitoring, and environmental sustainability.
